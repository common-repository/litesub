  <h1>How to Send Newsletters with Litesub</h1>
  <em><span style="color: #808080;">This is a sample newsletter from Litesub, and also a guide on newsletters.</span></em>

  Litesub can help you create newsletters in the same way you publish a post. First, compose a newsletter. Go to<strong> Litesub &gt; Newsletters &gt; Add New.</strong> Use the editor to design your newsletter.

  And you can use shortcode - <strong>litesub_newsletter_posts</strong>, to add your posts into a newsletter. For example,  you can place the shorcode here, like this.

  [litesub_newsletter_posts]

  Then select a few posts in the <strong>Choose your posts to be listed in this newsletter</strong> box, below this editor box. If you save this draft, and click the button <strong>Send a preview newsletter to me</strong>, a preview newsletter will be send to your email address.

  If the newsletter looks great to you and ready to be published, just click <strong>Publish, </strong>and it will be delivered to your subscribers.
  <p style="background-color: #eee; padding: 5px 10px; font-size: 14px;"><b>tips</b>
  Litesub will try to show your newsletters with the active Wordpress theme. If you are familiar with HTML &amp; CSS, you edit in text mode and apply more styles or tweaks to your newsletter. For example, add background colors, fonts, button-like links, etc.</p>
  <strong>Unsubscribe links</strong> will be appended to the bottom of your newsletters, don't worry about it.
